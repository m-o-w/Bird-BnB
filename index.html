About Us

Specialists in Hand-Raised Cockatiels & Parrots

Love For Cockatiels is built on a passion for raising happy, healthy, human-friendly birds. We hand-raise every bird from a young age, ensuring they are tame, social, and ready to become a loving companion in your home.

While cockatiels are our specialty, we also raise and offer other well-known parrots including Love Birds, Conures, African Greys, and Macaws. Every bird from us is nurtured with proper nutrition, socialization, and veterinary care.
