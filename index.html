About Us

Delhi NCR's Trusted Hand-Raised Cockatiel & Parrot Breeder

Love For Cockatiels is built on a passion for raising happy, healthy, hand-tamed pet birds. Every cockatiel and parrot is hand-fed and socialised from a young age, so they grow up tame, friendly, and ready to become a loving companion in your home.

While hand-raised cockatiels are our specialty, we also offer well-known pet parrots including lovebirds, conures, African greys, and macaws. Each bird is nurtured with proper nutrition, daily handling, and veterinary care — so you take home a healthy, human-friendly companion.
